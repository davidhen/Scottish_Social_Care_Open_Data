Welcome

Welcome to my research website showing some simple analyses with open data on social care published by the Scottish Government.

The social care survey is collected anually by the Scottish Government from all of the 32 Local Authorities in Scotland. Full history of data collection and metadata for the public release files is available here.

Public release data is for 3 years - 2010, 2011, and 2012 and is avaiable here. These are three files the analysis will predominantly use. Below are links to different stages in the analysis.
